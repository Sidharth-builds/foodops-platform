How Food Rescue Works

Simple steps. Big impact. Together we can end hunger.

1. Providers Post Surplus Food

Restaurants, hotels, and caterers list surplus food details on our platform before it goes to waste. This helps reduce food loss and save perfectly good meals from landfills.

2. NGOs Get Notified Instantly

Nearby verified NGOs receive real-time alerts about available food. They can claim the food immediately, ensuring fast and efficient pickup coordination.

3. Food Is Delivered to Those in Need

The food is collected and distributed to shelters, orphanages, and families who need it most, completing the cycle of kindness and sustainability.
