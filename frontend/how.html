How Food Rescue Works

Simple steps. Big impact.

1. Providers Post Surplus Food

Restaurants, hotels and caterers list surplus food before it goes to waste. This helps reduce food loss and save perfectly good meals.

2. NGOs Get Notified Instantly

Nearby NGOs receive alerts and can accept the food in real time, ensuring fast and efficient pickup.

3. Food Is Delivered to Those in Need

The food is collected and delivered to shelters, orphanages, and families who need it most.
